Hello to Impact.

Why not save the world from total annihilation!?

In the world of Impact, you are challenged to save the planet from a cometary extinction event.

As the player. you assume control over our chief scientist. You are tasked to deploy as many protective shields
around the planet as you can before time runs out to save our planet.

 

This planet is our home and protecting it is our responsibility, whether it is about sustainability...
or as in this case, total instantaneous extinction by meteors.


While this scenario is very, very unlikely to happen in real life the possibility is still there, and this simulator could
help you prepare for such disasters. While also having fun with it. I hope. 

Please check out Scottish Wildlife Trust for further information on how you could contribute
to sustaining our planets health on a local level.

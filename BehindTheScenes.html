1st Entry

Controller and Early Physics:
The physics of the game are designed to accommodate and include main game mechanics and features. As the player you are tasked to traverse around a planet and place protective shields that stop approaching meteors in their tracks. The movement is designed to be intuitive yet exciting with simulated gravity towards the centre of a 2D Planet.

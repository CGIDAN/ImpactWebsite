About the Developer

I am a young developer currently attending my first year of an HND course at Fife College in video game development. My background is in Photography/Photogrammetry and Animation/VFX. My interests in video game development grew with the more I learned about general 3D development and design.

I have always loved playing and tinkering with video games. PC games have been a major part of my life since I was a kid. I have always felt lucky to be born in an era where computers exist. Getting engaged in game development has been a major influence on my interests and so the learning began. While there is still a long path ahead of me, overcoming each challenge brings me closer to my goal of working in the video game industry full time.

Please check out the Behind The Scenes Page on this website to see more of the progress of this endeavour.
